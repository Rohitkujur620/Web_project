FACILITIES

Our role is to promote sports and physical fitness throughout the college. Detailed information about the sports infrastructure, coaches, sportsmen, sports organisations, sports schools, hostels is the sections below

AEROBICS

Are You Ready to Get Transformed? We provide training in our studio or online on Google meet lose Extra Weight / Body Fat, Our Aerobics & Diet program will help you in achieving your goals.

ARCHERY

Vision of Netaji Subhas Archey Club is to promote archery and produce archers who can win medals for India in Olympics or in international archery tournaments. We’re aiming for the future. Join us and experience the excitement of archery.

BADMINTON

The Netaji Subhas Shuttlers is one of the best academies for badminton in the city. Our sports facility has 6 synthetic courts with state-of-the-art facilities.

BASKETBALL

Basketball is a universal language. Whether you shoot hoops to let off steam or have dreams of an NBA future, Netaji Subhas outdoor basketball courts are where the true magic of the game happens.

CHILDREN'S PARK AND PLAYGROUND

We are north India's largest amusement and water park. Boating, rain dance and a video game arcade are just some of the attractions in addition to the rides here. You can also have your birthday party here to make it a birthday you'll remember forever!

CRICKET

Netaji Subhas Cricket Academy is a premier learning center for acquiring skills and techniques of the game which has religion like passion in India – Cricket.We have emerged as one of the most popular and respected cricket academies of Delhi and NCR.

FITNESS CENTER AND GYMNASIUM

We are a part of one of the world’s biggest fitness franchises,Netaji Subhas is a health club that pioneers in some of the revolutionary fitness techniques of Les Mills like Body Combat, RPM, Body Pump and Body Balance.

FOOTBALL

We offer state-of-the-art AstroTurf or artificial green pitches. With grounds like 6-a-side and caged pitches for an exhilarating experience, we have plenty of viewer space a cafeteria, washrooms, parking space and more for a pleasurable practice for the football fanatics.

JOGGING TRACK

Most runners prefer our place for a great morning run as the evergreen environment of this city forest seems like a perfect place. Our place dwells in the finest beauty of nature apart from that it is also home to various species of birds. Our area is also termed as haunted by localities so a morning run should be ideal. It's advisable to run here in small groups as per safety concerns.

MARTIAL ARTS

Fan of the martial arts or want to learn self-defence? There are tons of places in the city you can go to to learn but we are the best. We offer flexible timings and excellent trainers. We teach martial arts with a combination of traditional and modern training methods.

TENNIS

Netaji Subhas Sports Complex is one of the best places for all the tennis enthusiasts in town. We have three tennis courts - Clay Court, Synthetic Court and a mini court where kids below the age of 8 can also swing the racquets around.

YOGA

The holistic environment that we provide is well suited for those looking for a fit inner core to build on inner strength apart from fitness outside while giving the much-desired flexibility. Be prepared to be nurtured by the warmth and peaceful environment to work out and de-stress.
